Peer pressure



Types of peer pressure


Spoken pressure

When somebody physically askes you to do something that makes you do something that you might not want to do.
This is when someone...

  • Tells you that there will be an end to their friendship if they won't join in
  • Tries to persuade you to do something that you don't want to do
  • Is rude to you so that you will do something

Unspoken pressure

Unspoken pressure is when nobody actually says anything other are doing things and you think that you should too.
This might happen when...

  • Someone popular does something and so you do it too
  • People make their thinking obvious
